  -ABOUT ME-------

Hi! My name is Lily Liu and I'm a Chinese Canadian teenager currently living in Toronto.

Age: 16 (turning 17 on December 3rd!)
Favorite Movie(s): Interstellar, Imitation Game
Favorite Food(s): Fresh Spring Rolls (not the fried ones), Youtiao (the fried ones)
Least Favorite Food(s): ANYTHING with Alfredo sauce, Kraft Dinner
Bucket List: Christmas in NYC
Favorite Subjects: Math, Computer Science, Chemistry
Least Favorite Subjects: English
Favorite Books: Percy Jackson and the Olympians, Perks of Being a Wallflower


    A s I'm sure any other second-gen asian immigrant understands, I was used to hearing phrases from my classmates like "why does your food smell so weird?". Little experiences like these made it difficult for me to find appreciation and love for my culture. However, with time, introspection, and a few special people, I gradually began to recognize the beauty and strength in embracing my unique identity.
    So I created this "blog review website" whatever you want to call it, in hopes that I can share the amazing (and maybe not so amazing) East Asian restaurants/shopping/cafe spots that I've visited throughout my life.
