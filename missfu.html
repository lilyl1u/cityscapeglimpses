Lily's Point of View

Pro-tip, when they say level 1 spicy here, they really mean level 5 spicy everywhere else. In one of my dishes, I probably counted more spicy peppers than anything else. I've had authentic Szechuan food countless times before, but this was something else. Now I love spicy food, but I draw my line at spicy, when the spice overtakes and smothers all the other flavours. They other a mini hotpot here as well but even that was very mediocre. I definetly wouldn't come to this restaurant again.
