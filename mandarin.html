Lily's Point of View

Every single P.A. day growing up, you could find my brother, dad and I at the Mandarin buffet on Eglinton. I have gone to this buffet so many times that I have a strict regimen of what order to eat my food, in order to maximize food consumption. I always start with vegetables and salads, not the caesar salad though because the lettuce takes up too much volume in my stomach. Next I hop on over to the second row where the real food starts. I typically don't get into the meaty foods just yet but I might take a couple of fried shrimps or fish filets. I probably get two plates from the salad/veggie bar and another one or two from the second row of food. The third row of food is where the money lies. The barbeque ribs, smoked salmon, fried chicken, you name it. I typically max out in this row with another 3 plates. After that I’m usually pretty stuffed so I go to the soup bar to soothe my stomach a little bit. I always get the wonton soup, but only the broth part, not the actual wontons (trust me on that one). I like to think of my soup bowl as my rest time before the eating starts again. Next I hit the rows on the sides with the pizza, sushi, and mashed potatoes. I don’t like finishing my meal with deserts because I know for a fact they just buy those cookies and cakes from the Walmart nearby. If I have enough stomach left, I’ll go for another plate of veggies or I’ll steal something from my dads plate.
