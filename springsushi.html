Spring Sushi

10 Dundas St E #500, ON M5B 2G9 +

$$ · Japanese & AYCE

Lily's Point of View

So to be completely frank, when it comes to buffets and AYCEs, I am a little biased. I always eat like a pig at buffets so I do get more worth out of my food compared to other restuarants. Then does that mean that buffets are always more worth it? I don't know. So to be fair, I'll compare Spring Sushi to other buffets rather than other restaurants. In terms of ambience and vibe, Spring Sushi is amazing. Their patio rooftop overlooking Yonge & Dundas Square (take that as you may) is an unbeatable location. I guess that justifies their higher than average prices. Lunch for 29.99 and dinner for 38.99 isn't the most glamourous price but they do have an amazing menu. In terms of food quality, I honestly can't tell the difference between good and bad sushi, so whether that price is reasonable is up to you to go and try. One thing about sushi buffets that I always recommend is to go with a larger group of friends. Sushi rolls usually come in 6-8 pieces so if you went with just one person, you would have to each eat 3 or 4 of the same flavour, versus if you went with multiple you could do small samplings instead and order more if you want.
