$ · Dim Sum

Lily's Point of View

Over the years Rol San has definetly downgraded in terms of quality and price in my opinion. Although the selection of food here is practically limitless, I just think there are lots more restaurants in Chinatown worth your time and money. There isnt anything special about Rol San that you wouldn't be able to find anywhere else. However if you do happen to stumble across this restaurant I would highly recommend their fried eggplant or beef shortribs.
