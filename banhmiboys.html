Lily's Point of View

I only just figured out recently that the owner of Banh Mi Boys is the related to the owner of Banh Mi Nguyen Huong on Spadina! Now I had to try this modern fast-food take on Banh Mi. To be honest, it tasted the same as any other Banh Mi place I've tried. I always wish they went a little heavier on the picked carrots and vegetables because I find that it counteracts and somewhat neutralizes the oiliness of the Vietnamese butter and the cold cuts. My opinion may be a little biased though because to be honest if you gave me a sandwich with just the picked vegetables I would be more than happy. Taste-wise I couldn't tell a huge difference between this place and Nguyen Huong, I mean after all the owners probably live in one house, so I would say if you were to try any Banh Mi place in Toronto, Banh Mi Nguyen Huong is still your spot.
