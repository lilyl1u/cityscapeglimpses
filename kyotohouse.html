Kyoto House

143 Dundas St W, M5G 1C5

$ · Japanese + AYCE

Lily's Point of View

I was genuinely sitting in my room contemplating whether I should gatekeep this place or not. In the heart of downtown Toronto just a 5 minute walk away from the Eaton Centre sits a nice little restuarant on the end of the block with TWENTY DOLLARS ALL YOU CAN EAT SUSHI?? Maybe Kyoto was reported for like health issues a while back because why on Earth was there barely anybody here when I went? I didn't even need to make reservations or anything. But you already know I had to try the entire menu. Now I wouldnt say the sushi served here was anything out of this world, but to be honest I cannot taste the difference between 5 dollar sushi and 30 dollar sushi. I think for a food like sushi to really taste different, you would need to go to the 60$+ restaurants. Generally a roll of sushi at a grocery store or food-court would be around 9-10 dollars, and I had sushi rolls in the double digits let me tell you. That's like 2$/an ENTIRE ROLL, plus all the other dishes they served, such as their udon noodles, terryaki chicken, and matcha ice cream to name a few. I would say Kyoto is 100% worth going to especially in times like these were a 20$ buffet is almost impossible to stumble upon.
